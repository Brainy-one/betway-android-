Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Betway Focus Timer, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Pomodoro productivity timer application.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Batway Focus Timer is designed to work completely offline and does not transmit any information to external servers.

What We DON'T Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies or similar tracking technologies

2. Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This includes:

  • Timer settings (focus duration, break duration, etc.)
  • Your productivity statistics (completed sessions, focus time)
  • Streak tracking data
  • Theme preferences
  • Timer state (current time, phase, session count)

Important: This data never leaves your device. It is stored in your browser's local storage and is only accessible by the app on your device. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or uninstalling the app.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services. Batway Focus Timer does not integrate with:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • External CDNs (all resources are local)

4. Offline Functionality

Batway Focus Timer is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works completely offline after the initial installation. The app uses a service worker to cache all necessary files locally, ensuring:

  • No internet connection required after first load
  • No network requests to external servers
  • All functionality available offline
  • Complete data privacy

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect or transmit any information, the security of your data depends on:

  • Your device's security measures
  • Your browser's security features
  • Your operating system's protection

We recommend keeping your device and browser updated with the latest security patches.

6. Children's Privacy

Batway Focus Timer does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages as it operates entirely offline and stores data only on the user's device.

7. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app interface
  • Modify: Change settings and preferences at any time
  • Delete: Clear browser storage or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: Data stays on your device; no export needed

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Microsoft Store

If you download Batway Focus Timer from the Microsoft Store, Microsoft may collect certain information as part of their platform services. This collection is governed by Microsoft's Privacy Policy, not ours. We do not have access to any data Microsoft may collect.

For information about Microsoft's data practices, please visit: https://privacy.microsoft.com/

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@batwayfocustimer.com

Privacy Summary

  • Zero data collection
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No ads or third-party services
  • Complete offline functionality
  • All data stays on your device
  • You have full control over your data
